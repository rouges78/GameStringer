GameStringer only modifies text/localization files offline, doesn't alter gameplay, doesn't bypass DRM and doesn't communicate with game servers. It's equivalent to installing a translation mod. For single-player offline games there's no risk. For online games, always check the specific EULA - some companies prohibit any modification.
GameStringer automatically installs BepInEx (modding framework) and XUnity.AutoTranslator (translation plugin). It extracts all text from the game, translates with your preferred AI, and creates translation files. The game will automatically load translations. All with one click, zero manual configuration.
